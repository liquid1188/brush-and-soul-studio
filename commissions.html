Good to know

How long does a commission take?

It depends on size and detail — most pieces take a few weeks once your spot in the studio is reserved. You'll get a timeline with your quote.

What raises the price above the base?

Working on canvas, intricate and highly detailed compositions, and embellishments — jewels, crystals, stones, gold leaf, resin, or glitter worked into the piece. Framing can be added too. Everything is confirmed before any painting begins.

What media does Caroline paint in?

Primarily watercolor and alcohol ink, often layered with embellishments for depth and shimmer. If you're not sure what suits your idea, just ask in the form and she'll recommend.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. Caroline paints from Iran and ships worldwide; shipping is quoted with your piece based on size and destination.

Can I commission a specific subject or place?

Absolutely — a courtyard, a garden, a portrait in her style. Share it in the form and she'll tell you what's possible.
